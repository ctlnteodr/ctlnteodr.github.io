A little bit of history.

The band was founded in Stockholm in 2013. Thaiboy Digital was at the time residing in Sweden. Bladee and Ecco2K were previously in the grindcore duo Krossad. Drain Gang signed to the YEAR0001 label and rose to prominence in the cloud rap scene through collaborations with labelmate Yung Lean.

Bladee explains the meaning of the group's name:

"#Drain is about loss and gain; it could be good or bad — you could be drained of energy or you could drain something to gain energy. There’s financial, emotional and physical drains, for example — you could just be draining your bank account at the store. It doesn’t have to be deep. Basically, if I’m talking about ‘eating the night’ that means I drain it for its essence. Everything me and my bros do is connected to that concept — we might drain some blood for good fortune."

Thaiboy Digital's visa expired in 2015, and he was deported from Sweden. He has been able to continue collaborating with the other members from his new home in Bangkok thanks to the internet. In 2016, he and Bladee released AvP, a collaborative EP that featured production from both Whitearmor and Yung Sherman.

Bladee, Ecco2K, and Thaiboy Digital collaborated on the album D&G, which featured production from member Whitearmor as well as frequent collaborators Gud, Yung Sherman, Woesum, and Australian-based collective Ripsquadd.

Trash Island, the group's collaborative EP, was released in 2019. Ripsquadd and German producer Mechatok returned to contribute additional production, while Whitearmor served as executive producer. The EP came as a surprise, as no announcement was made on YEAR0001's or the group's various social media platforms.

The group announced a 2022 Drain Gang world tour in late 2021, which will include 23 performances across Europe and North America.

Bladee and Ecco2K collaborated on a single called "Amygdala" in 2022, which was produced by Mechatok.

Crest, a collaborative album by Bladee and Ecco2K, was released unannounced in March of the same year. Whitearmor produced the entire album, which included the single "Girls Just Want to Have Fun."

Bladee - Drain Story

Bladee's first solo release since his 2021 album The Fool is "DRAIN STORY." Over a catchy instrumental, Bladee describes relationship issues to her significant other, comparing their love to a story.

The song was released as a surprise, with no prior notice. The Ecco2k-directed, edited, and filmed music video, which features Bladee in the streets of Stockholm, Sweden, was released on the same day as the song.
